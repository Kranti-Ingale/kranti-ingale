My ResearchResearch

My research investigates how to make address translation scale on massively parallel GPUs. Modern GPUs stress the TLB hierarchy with thousands of concurrent threads; I design coalesced, multi-level TLB structures and page-walk mechanisms that reduce translation overhead, and evaluate them on cycle-accurate simulators.

GPU Address Translation

TLB hierarchies, coalescing, and page-walk optimization for high-throughput GPUs.

Memory Systems & Caching

Cache management, prefetching, and the memory hierarchy of CPUs and accelerators.

Architectural Simulation

Cycle-accurate modeling and evaluation with MGPUSim (AMD GCN3) and ChampSim.
